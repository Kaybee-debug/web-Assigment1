Welcome to my Home

Home is where the heart is’ — is a popular proverb and home in a village
is more comfortable than a town. My home is in a village and it is made
of brick, wood, and tin. Such kind of home is popularly known as half building.
There are five members in my family. They are parents, aunts, me, and my younger
sister, Ankhi. As a proud member of my family, I can say that my home is comfortable.

Here everybody has a separate rest room ,I have a separate study room also and we share Kitchen
I like my house very much because it meets every need of its members.
I am thankful for my home because when I grow up, I can look back at the
wonderful memories I made here. The walk down the memory lane will be a sweet
one because of the safety and security my home has given me. It is indeed an ideal home.
